# Operators
  
| Operator | Description                 |
|:---------|:----------------------------|
|.         |Scope                        |
|[]        |Subscript                    |
|<<        |Bitwise left shift           |
|>>        |Bitwise right shift          |
|\*        |Multiplication               |
|/         |Division                     |
|%         |Remainder                    |
|+         |Addition                     |
|-         |Subtraction                  |
|\|        |Bitwise OR                   |
|&         |Bitwise AND                  |
|^         |Bitwise XOR                  |
|~         |Binary ones complement       |
|>>>       |Unsigned right shift         |
